***Dora:*** *How does one orientate in the desert, then? If everything is always changing. How does one leave?*

***Nox:*** *You follow the horizon. Day by day, night by night. But only in light can you pass safely.*

---

FTH is a part of the **Different Lives** storyline. 

What was supposed to be a 2 day trip to find a certain palace where Qadar school once was turns out to be a 3 week long crusade in the desert heat...all kinds of heat really.

After being lost for a decent amount of time, as is Dora's custom, just before she is to be teared to shreds and presumably eaten by a deadly desert creature, she is found and (sort of) rescued by a Shinkanai Desert Nomad. The said nomad brings her to a secret Shinkanai Army base. Under the watchful eyes of the Shinkanai military, Dora searches both for answers and an exit but things aren't as simple as they seem, especially since she finds there is one pair of eyes she doesn't mind being watched by...

***A short interlude from the main story full of intrigue, mysticism and hot chinamen.***
